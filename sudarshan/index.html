How does the magic work?

Sudarshan makes cleaning up the environment easy and fun. While you jog from point A to point B, you can collect litter along the way. The app suggests smart trash bins equipped with sensors and displays for convenient waste disposal. Drop in the waste, scan the bin's QR code using the app, and your heroic efforts are recorded.

Got Questions?

What does the generated QR code do?

The QR code, on scanning, rewards you for your eco-friendly actions with points which can be redeemed as cash reward or shopping vouchers.

Are the smart trash bins secure?

Absolutely! Our smart trash bins use TOTP key to encrypt a JWT which is refreshed every 30 seconds. This architecture ensures user safety, proper disposal and offline support.

Where can I download Sudarshan?

Currently you can follow the setup instruction mention in README.md for setting up Sudarshan in your mobile devies.

Is Sudarshan compatible with all devices?

Absolutely! Sudarshan is designed to work flawlessly on iOS and Android.
